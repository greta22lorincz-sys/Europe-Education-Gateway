Services

Service packages

We work with a clear, written agreement: you sign a contract with Europe Education Gateway that lists every deliverable below. Our practical support is centred on Oradea and programmes such as the University of Oradea (and comparable options where relevant).

We guide you through contacting the university — you remain the applicant; we do not present ourselves as your legal representative to the institution.

Presenting study options

Structured overview of realistic choices, with emphasis on University of Oradea and related pathways, so you understand entry routes, language, and campus fit before you commit.

Requirements explained

We translate official criteria into a simple list: grades, language proof, deadlines, fees, and any programme-specific extras.

Step-by-step application guidance

Milestone-based support from portal registration to final upload — including what to save, what to double-check, and when to hit submit.

Help contacting the university

Drafts, talking points, and follow-up discipline — always with the understanding that you are the party writing or signing correspondence, and we are your coach, not your proxy.

Document checklist & review

Line-by-line checklist plus a consistency review (names, dates, scans) so your file looks professional the first time.

Exam & interview preparation guidance

What to expect, how to structure answers, and how to connect your documents to what assessors care about.

Timeline planning

One shared calendar covering applications, document legalisation, travel, enrolment, and residence steps — adjusted to your intake.

Accommodation assistance in Oradea

Hands-on help securing housing in Oradea, plus orientation for everyday setup:

  • Bank account setup
  • SIM card
  • Notary visits
  • Residency guidance
In-person support during the first days

We meet practical needs on the ground — keys, appointments, local routines — so you are not solving everything alone on day one.

Contract & pricing

Every client receives a written contract describing the package, exclusions, refund or termination clauses (if any), and our mutual responsibilities before work begins.

If something is not in the contract, it is not part of the agreed mandate — that keeps expectations transparent on both sides.
