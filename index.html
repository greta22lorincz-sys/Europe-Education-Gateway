Your gateway to a European education

A degree in Europe can open real doors—but the path from where you are today to a lecture hall abroad is rarely a straight line. Europe Education Gateway exists so non-EU students do not have to navigate it alone: we translate complexity into clear next steps, in plain language, with your long-term study goals in view.

We are especially strong on Romanian universities and student life—think pathways such as the University of Oradea and settling in Oradea—while keeping the bigger picture of your European future in mind. Before we support you along the way, we put everything in a written contract.

Why work with us

Your pathway to a European education

We help non-EU students pursue degrees in Romania with clarity and structure — with a strong focus on study options such as the University of Oradea and similar pathways. Before any work begins, you sign a contract with us so the scope of support, timelines, and deliverables are agreed in writing.

Romania offers internationally recognized degrees and approachable living costs. We work directly from Romania — a real advantage when you need timely answers, local know-how, and support that matches how universities and public offices actually operate here. Our role is to guide you through requirements, applications, and arrival — including hands-on help in Oradea — while you remain the applicant in direct contact with the institution (we do not represent you legally before the university).
